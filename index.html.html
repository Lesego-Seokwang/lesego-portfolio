About the Journalist

Lesego Seokwang is a skilled multimedia journalist with more than a decade of experience in reporting, editing, public relations, and content management. She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism from the University of Johannesburg and has contributed to major South African publications including The Citizen, YOU Magazine, and News24. Lesego’s work spans crime and court reporting, human-interest features, governance and policy analysis, and lifestyle storytelling.
